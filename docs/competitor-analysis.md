# Competitor Analysis - Danish Christmas Tree Export

## Top Players (Page 1 Google)

### Tier 1 - Professional/Corporate

**Dalgas** (dalgas.com)
- Part of larger forestry/land management group
- Very professional, corporate website
- Clear team with photos and direct contact
- ESG/sustainability messaging
- Private label services
- Multiple tree species + greenery
- **Takeaway:** Corporate polish, clear contacts, sustainability angle

**Green Product A/S** (green-product.dk)
- Clear grading system (1st, 2nd, 3rd, ungraded)
- Educational content (videos about Nordmann fir history)
- Professional photography
- Emphasis on quality control by foresters
- **Takeaway:** Educational approach, clear product hierarchy

**Dane Christmas Tree** (danechristmastree.com)
- Production-focused (200 hectares, 200k+ trees/year)
- Multiple certifications prominently displayed (Naturbaum, Global GAP, GRASP)
- "From seed to delivery" story
- Aims to be "TOP 10 producer"
- Just-in-time delivery emphasis
- **Takeaway:** Certifications, vertical integration story, growth ambition

### Tier 2 - Established B2B

**Geil & Berner A/S** (geil-berner.dk)
- "One of Denmark's major exporters" - since 1989
- Simple website but clear message
- Global GAP + Certified Original Nordmann
- "Excellence and precision in logistics"
- **Takeaway:** Credibility through scale, certification, logistics focus

**Birkholm Christmas Trees** (birkholm-christmastrees.dk)
- 25+ years experience, exporting since 2003
- B2B focus, trades AND manufactures
- Export to Europe, Russia, Asia
- Custom branding/labeling offered
- Personal touch - invites customers to visit
- **Takeaway:** Trading + production, international reach, personal service

**NordmannTrees** (nordmanntrees.com)
- Equipment sales angle (packing funnels, etc.)
- Customer testimonials
- Looks like a newer/smaller player
- **Takeaway:** Niche positioning possible

### Industry Portal

**christmastree-trading.com**
- Directory of Danish producers
- Silva Green is listed here
- Good for visibility

**Danish Christmas Tree Association** (christmastree.dk)
- Official association
- 2,500 growers, 11 million trees/year
- "Find producer" search function

## Competitive Gaps & Opportunities

### What competitors emphasize:
1. Certifications (Global GAP, Naturbaum, GRASP)
2. Own production/vertical integration
3. Quality grading systems
4. Logistics reliability
5. Years in business

### What's MISSING from most competitors:
1. **Modern, tech-forward presentation** - Most sites look dated
2. **Clear team/leadership visibility** - Few show actual people
3. **Speed and flexibility messaging** - "We can produce whatever you need fast"
4. **Pan-European sourcing network** - Most focus only on Danish production
5. **Systems and process transparency** - Nobody shows their operational sophistication
6. **Greenery as equal product line** - Often buried or secondary
7. **Next-generation story** - All about founders, none about succession

## Market Data

- Denmark: Europe's largest Christmas tree exporter
- 10-12 million trees exported annually
- €134 million total export value
  - €107 million Christmas trees
  - €27 million greenery
- Germany takes 49% of exports
- France, UK, Netherlands are other major markets
- 2,500 growers (down from 3,500 in 2012)
- Consolidation trend - smaller producers exiting
