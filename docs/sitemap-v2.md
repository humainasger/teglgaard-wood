## Key Messaging Throughout

### Headlines to use:
- "Your Professional Christmas Tree Partner"
- "Full Production. European Network. 50 Years Expertise."
- "Source. Produce. Deliver."
- "Quality Trees, Reliable Supply"
- "Greenery Specialists"

### Trust signals:
- 50 years in the industry
- European grower network
- Full production team in Denmark
- Flexible and fast delivery
- Long-term customer relationships

### Avoid:
- Over-emphasis on founder's personal story
- "Family business" feel - go professional
- Outdated claims (250k trees - use current accurate numbers)
- Silva Green references
